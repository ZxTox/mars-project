Who are We?

The name of our company is 'MarsMile' and consists of 5 founders: Bosmans Randy, Defoor Kobe, Deschoolmeester Dries, Fida Djelal and Lemaire Milan.

MarsMile wants to offer sustainable transport for inhabitants of Earth and Mars who would like to travel between the two planets for visiting their family and friends.

The distance between the two planets is 54.6 million kilometers at their closest point and 401 million kilometers at their maximum distance. We specialize in covering this great distance comfortably, sustainably and efficiently, hence our name 'MarsMile'.

We want to make sure that martians and earthlings can travel in comfort and arrive at their destination without any problems.
